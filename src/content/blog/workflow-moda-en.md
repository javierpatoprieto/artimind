An AI fashion campaign isn't asking a model for one image and crossing your fingers. It's a process. This is mine.

## 1. Art direction first

Before generating anything, I define palette, light, framing and model reference. The clearer the direction, the fewer iterations later.

## 2. Model and consistency

I lock a reference model so every look feels like the same shoot. Consistency is what separates a professional catalog from a pile of loose images.

## 3. Production and variations

I generate each look, check hands, fabrics and reflections, and produce the format variations the brand needs: editorial, vertical and product page.

## 4. Finishing with Magnific

The final upscaling is what delivers print quality. This is where the image goes from good to campaign.
