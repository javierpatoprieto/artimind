A good product prompt doesn't describe the product: it describes the photograph. These are the five keys with the most impact.

## 1. Light before the object

Define the type of light (soft, hard, side) before describing the product. Light is 80% of the result.

## 2. Material and finish

"Satin glass", "brushed metal", "matte plastic". The material drives the reflections and texture.

## 3. Background and surface

A neutral background sells ecommerce; a textured surface sells campaign. Decide what you're playing.

## 4. Optics

Specifying focal length and aperture ("85mm, f/2.8") controls depth and the character of the image.

## 5. Brand reference

Attaching a reference image aligns the result with your identity better than a thousand words.
