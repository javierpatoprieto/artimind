A ready-to-wear collection needed a campaign before launch, with no time for an international shoot. I created a full editorial with AI models, consistent styling and a unified art direction.

## What it included

- Art direction and style references.
- 12 final looks in editorial and vertical social formats.
- Background and framing variations for web and ecommerce.

The result: a campaign ready in days, with the consistency of a single shoot and the freedom to iterate any frame instantly.
