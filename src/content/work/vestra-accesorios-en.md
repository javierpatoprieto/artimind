An accessories brand needed to refresh its entire catalog with one visual language. I produced the pieces on-model and in still life, keeping lighting and framing consistent across the collection.

## What it included

- Bags and footwear on-model and on a clean background.
- A consistent visual system for the whole product page.
- Delivery in their ecommerce platform formats.

A full catalog with a consistency impossible to achieve by stitching together shoots from different days.
